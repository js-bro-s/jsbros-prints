## Pricing Guide

| Size | Time | Price Range |
|------|------|-------------|
| Tiny (&lt;30mm) | &lt;30 min | $0.50–$1.00 |
| Small (30–80mm) | 30 min–2 hrs | $1.00–$2.50 |
| Medium (80–150mm) | 2–5 hrs | $2.50–$4.00 |
| Large (150mm+) | 5+ hrs | $5.00+ |

**Cost formula:** Material cost + (print hours × $0.50) + $0.50 profit margin

## About Us

We're a group of kids who design, 3D print, and sell items at school. All designs are original — we don't copy other people's work. Every item is test-printed before it goes on sale, and if something breaks in under a week, we fix the design before selling again.
