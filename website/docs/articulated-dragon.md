# Articulated Dragon

A flexi dragon with a segmented body that bends and wiggles. Print-in-place — no assembly needed. The premium item in our catalog.

## Description

A single-print articulated dragon with 12 body segments that flex and move. The joints are printed in place — just peel it off the bed and start bending. Wings are flat-printed and fan out from the sides. The head has a small open mouth detail. This is our showstopper item and always sells fast.

## Print Notes

- Print-in-place: do NOT use supports or the joints will fuse
- Bed adhesion is critical — use a brim if your bed isn't perfectly level
- After printing, gently flex each joint to break it free
- 0.2mm layer height, slow speed (40mm/s) for clean joints
- Silk or multicolor PLA makes these pop
