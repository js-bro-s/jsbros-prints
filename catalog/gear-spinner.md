# Gear Spinner

A palm-sized gear mechanism with three interlocking gears that spin when you turn one. Mesmerizing to fidget with and cool to watch.

## Details

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Price** | $2.00 |
| **Print Time** | ~1.5 hours |
| **Material** | PLA |
| **Dimensions** | 65mm × 65mm × 10mm |
| **Infill** | 20% |
| **Supports** | No |

## Description

Three gears mounted on a flat base plate. Turn the center gear and the two outer gears spin in the opposite direction. The teeth mesh smoothly right off the print bed. Flat and pocket-friendly — toss it in your backpack and fidget between classes.

## Print Notes

- Print flat on the bed — this is a print-in-place design
- Gears should spin freely after printing; if stuck, gently twist to free
- 0.2mm layer height with 0.15mm first layer for clean gear teeth
- Looks great in two-tone if you do a filament swap at layer 3
