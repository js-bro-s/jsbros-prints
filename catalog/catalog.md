## Pricing Guide

| Size | Time | Price Range |
|------|------|-------------|
| Tiny (<30mm) | <30 min | $0.50–$1 |
| Small (30–80mm) | 30 min–2 hrs | $1–$2 |
| Medium (80–150mm) | 2–5 hrs | $2–$4 |
| Large (150mm+) | 5+ hrs | $5+ |

**Cost formula:** Material cost + (print hours × $0.50) + $0.50 profit margin
