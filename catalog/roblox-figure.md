# Roblox Character Figure

A blocky mini figure inspired by the Roblox character style. Two-piece print (head + body) that snaps together. Customize with paint or markers.

## Details

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Price** | $3.00 |
| **Print Time** | ~2.5 hours |
| **Material** | PLA |
| **Dimensions** | 40mm × 25mm × 60mm |
| **Infill** | 20% |
| **Supports** | Yes (arms) |

## Description

The classic blocky character shape that every Roblox player recognizes. Printed in two parts — the head pops onto the body with a press-fit peg. Leave it plain or customize it with acrylic paint or Sharpies to match your avatar.

## Print Notes

- Print head and body separately
- Use supports on the arms — they're the trickiest part
- Glue after assembly if the press-fit is loose
- Offer a "paint your own" option for extra appeal
